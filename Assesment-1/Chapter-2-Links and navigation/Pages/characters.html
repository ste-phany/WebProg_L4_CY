Main Characters

Elphaba Thropp: The misunderstood and rebellious witch who becomes known as the Wicked Witch of the West.

Glinda: The bubbly and popular witch who is known as Glinda the Good Witch.

Madame Morrible: The headmistress of Shiz University who becomes a mentor and friend to Elphaba, at least at first.

Nessarose Thropp: The younger sister of Elphaba Thropp. She's paraplegic and desperate for independence.

Prince Fiyero: Elphaba's love interest and a former Arjiki prince-turned-chieftain from the Vinkus (or Winkie Country) who attends Shiz University. His family reside at Kiamo Ko, a castle in which Elphaba would go to live her final days.

Dr. Dillamond: Elphaba and Galinda's History teacher at Shiz University—the very last Animal teacher at Oz's prestigious school. He and Elphaba develop a friendship in the first Wicked movie built on the fact that they both know what it feels like to be ostracized.

Pfannee: A supporting character in the 2024 film, Wicked. He is one of "Galinda's conniving compatriots".

Governor Thropp: Father of Elphaba & Nessarose Thropp.

Mrs. Thropp: Mother of Elphaba & Nessarose Thropp

Professor Nikidik: He is Dr. Dillamond's replacement, as well as the teacher who seems to be responsible for taking the lion cub, Brrr, from his mother, which enrages Elphaba.

Boq: A Munchkin who befriends Elphaba and Glinda during their time at Shiz University. With a major crush on Glinda, he also becomes the love interest for Nessarose, Elphaba’s younger sister, for much of the story.

Shenshen: Madam Shenshen Minkos was one of Glinda's friends back when the latter was known was Galinda. She attended Shiz University the same time Elphaba attended.

The Wizard: The main antagonist in the Broadway musical, Wicked. He is a humbug ruler who uses deceit and trickery to hide his own mortal shortcomings.

Miss Coddle: She is the Head Mistress of Shiz University.

Shiz President: In the movie scene he'll be found, the one welcoming the students in the beginning of the film.
