Plot of Wicked 2025 Movie Series

Wicked is an adaptation of Gregory Maguire's 1995 novel, which is a reimagining of L. Frank Baum's "The Wizard of Oz" told from the perspective of the witches of Oz. The story follows the unlikely friendship between two witches, Elphaba and Glinda. Elphaba, born with green skin, is intelligent and misunderstood, eventually becoming the infamous Wicked Witch of the West. Glinda, on the other hand, is the bubbly and popular Good Witch of the North, known for her beauty and charm.
